This list contains the BBSs known to us which operate on a regular schedule in
Hartford's or Manchester's local calling area; all not ?-flagged were verified
02-01-91 or later.  However, since boards continually open and close, we need
YOUR feedback to keep the list current.  IF YOU KNOW OF ADDITIONS OR CHANGES,
PLEASE LEAVE A PUBLIC MESSAGE to DAVID FARRINGTON or DAN MILLER on the IVORY
TOWER (649-5611); Connecticut Yankee is temporarily down.  Interim copies which
reflect changes as we verify them are maintained in BULLETIN 8 on both boards.
